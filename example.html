jStars is a self-contained jQuery plugin for displaying star ratings. While there are many rating plugins available, most are heavy, complex, and require additional CSS/image files. The purpose of this plugin is to provide a simple, self-contained way to display star ratings on your website without all the extra baggage.

Basic Usage


<div class="jstars" data-value="3.0"></div>

Advanced Usage


<div class="jstars" data-value="4.2" data-total-stars="7" data-color="#22D118" data-empty-color="black" data-size="30px"></div>
